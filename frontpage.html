Hej! Welcome to your new life!


Hej! is an individual-oriented scheduler / reminder application which assists planning of an organization, and it is there solely for you. Hej! Provides visual feedback whether you have over-populated days with too many things by nullfying the overwhelming sensation of feeling too busy by mixing calming colors and activities together.




Event calendars and reminders

Input your own reminders in a clear schedule that mirror the habits you wish to develop. By organizing your inputs into categories that are color coded. HEJ! lets you know when to do your reminders in a clear and concise way.

An achievement system that rewards you!

The achievement system gives you encouragement through badges earned by doing the activities and encouraging statements, such as “you are doing great! Keep up the good work! Or a reminder, like “have you had your lunch yet?” We want to make setting realistic goals and accomplishing them fun for you! Think of Hej! like a game where you make the rules and you decide when you’ve won!
